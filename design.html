Effective Design Principles

Good design is critical for effective system architecture. Here are some essential principles to consider:

  • Simplicity: Keep designs as straightforward as possible.
  • Scalability: Ensure the system can grow and adapt over time.
  • Modularity: Use modular components that can be developed and updated independently.
  • Maintainability: Design for ease of updates and repairs.
